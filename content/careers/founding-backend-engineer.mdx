> Placeholder role. Illustrative until hiring formally opens.

We're looking for a founding backend engineer to build the core of Nebbos: the
Operational Graph that models how an organisation actually runs, and the
deterministic-detection and governed-action engine that turns its signal into
foresight. This is the layer that decides what's worth a thought and what isn't —
and that does the right thing only when a person has said it can.

## You might be a fit if

- You're fluent in Python and TypeScript and comfortable moving between them.
- You've done real data and graph modelling — relationships, thresholds, timing —
  not just CRUD.
- You care about governance, auditability, and showing the system's work, not
  hiding it behind a clever model.

Keep the human in the loop. Show your work. Earn trust slowly. If that resonates,
